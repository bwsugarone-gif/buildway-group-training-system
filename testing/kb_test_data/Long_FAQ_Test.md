## Ordering Process

### Step-by-Step Ordering Guide

**Step 1: Inquiry and Quotation**
Send us your product requirements including:
- Product name or description
- Quantity needed
- Customization requirements
- Target price range
- Delivery deadline

We will respond within 24 hours with a detailed quotation.

**Step 2: Sample Order**
We strongly recommend ordering samples before bulk production:
- Sample cost: $50-150 per item
- Sample production time: 5-7 days
- Shipping time: 3-5 days by express
- Sample fee is refundable on bulk orders of 500+ units

**Step 3: Sample Approval**
Review the samples and provide feedback:
- Approve as-is and proceed to bulk order
- Request modifications (additional sample fee may apply)
- Reject and explore alternative products

**Step 4: Proforma Invoice**
Once samples are approved, we will send:
- Proforma Invoice with full order details
- Payment instructions
- Production timeline
- Shipping arrangements

**Step 5: Deposit Payment**
Standard payment terms:
- 30% deposit to start production
- 70% balance before shipment
- Payment methods: T/T, L/C, PayPal (for small orders)

**Step 6: Production**
During production we provide:
- Production start notification
- Progress updates at 30%, 60%, 90% completion
- Pre-shipment photos for approval
- Quality inspection reports

**Step 7: Balance Payment and Shipment**
- Final inspection and approval
- Balance payment
- Shipping arrangement
- Tracking information provided
- Estimated delivery date

**Step 8: Delivery and After-Sales**
- Confirm receipt of goods
- Inspection period: 7 days
- Report any issues immediately
- After-sales support available

**Bank Transfer (T/T):**
- Most common method for international trade
- Lower transaction fees

**Letter of Credit (L/C):**

## Shipping and Logistics

### Shipping Methods

**Sea Freight:**
- Most economical for large orders
- Suitable for orders over 100kg
- Transit time: 20-35 days depending on destination
- Port-to-port or door-to-door service available

**Air Freight:**
- Faster than sea freight
- Suitable for urgent orders
- Transit time: 5-10 days
- Higher cost than sea freight

**Express Courier:**
- DHL, FedEx, UPS, TNT available
- Best for samples and small urgent orders
- Transit time: 3-5 days
- Door-to-door service with tracking

### Incoterms

We work with the following Incoterms:

**FOB (Free On Board):**
- We deliver goods to the ship at our port
- You arrange and pay for ocean freight
- You handle customs clearance at destination
- Most common for experienced importers

**CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight):**
- We arrange and pay for ocean freight to your port
- We purchase cargo insurance
- You handle customs clearance at destination
- Good balance of responsibility

**DDP (Delivered Duty Paid):**
- We handle everything including customs and delivery to your door
- Highest cost but most convenient
- Recommended for first-time importers
- Available for most destinations

### Packaging

**Standard Packaging:**
- Individual poly bag or bubble wrap
- Inner box (white or color box)
- Master carton with shipping marks
- Pallet packaging for large orders

**Custom Packaging:**
- Retail-ready packaging

## Quality Control and Certifications

### Quality Control Process

**Incoming Material Inspection:**
- All raw materials inspected upon arrival
- Material certificates verified
- Sample testing for critical components
- Rejected materials returned to suppliers

**In-Process Quality Control:**
- Random inspection during production
- Critical checkpoints at 30%, 60%, 90% completion
- Immediate correction of any defects
- Production photos sent to customers

**Pre-Shipment Inspection:**
- 100% visual inspection
- Random functional testing (AQL 2.5 standard)
- Packaging inspection
- Carton drop test
- Final approval before shipment

**Third-Party Inspection:**
- SGS, BV, Intertek, TUV available
